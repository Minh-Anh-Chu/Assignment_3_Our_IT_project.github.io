Group processes

We will conduct a thorough analysis of our project's concept, which is the cyber security and its virtual environment, over the next several weeks in order to provide a more complete review. This was accomplished by dividing the crew in half and assigning each half to a distinct component of the project: The project team where all the project details and information including how the system function and design of the project. The website team where all the information provided in the project will be included in the website.
